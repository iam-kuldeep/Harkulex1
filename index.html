Who We Are: The Harkulex Story

Harkulex Automations was born from a shared vision of providing reliable, high-quality industrial and electronic supplies. Founded by **Kuldeep Kumar** and **Harsh Shreshtha Singh**, we are a young company driven by passion and a commitment to streamlining supply chains for businesses across India.

Our primary focus is serving **Business-to-Business (B2B)** industrial clients with customized solutions and bulk ordering capabilities, while also offering direct support to **Business-to-Consumer (B2C)** individual buyers and hobbyists.
